** Over View **
* This project explores a large Amazon dataset containing over 1.4 million products listings.
* The analysis investigate pricing,customer ratings,reviews,purchasing trends,and best sellers
* performance to uncover insights into products succees on the Amazon market place.

  ** Dataset Features **
  * Asin *
  * Product Title *
  * Product Image URL *
  * Product URL *
  * Customers Ratings *
  * Number of Reviews *
  * Price *
  * List Price *
  * Category ID *
  * Best Seller Status *
  * Purchase in the Last Month *

    ** Objectives **

    * Explore product pricing patterns *
    * Analyze customer ratings and reviews *
    * Compare  best sellind and non best selling  products *
    * Identify non best selling products *
    * Examine relationships between key products meyrics *
    * Explore catagory level products distribution *
   
      ** Tools Used **
      * Python *
      * Pandas *
      * Numpy *
      * Seaborn *
      * Matplotlib *
      * Jupiter notebook *
     
      ** Key Insight **
      ** Most Amazon products are price belowe $50 **
      ** Best seller products receive significantly more review and purchases than non best sellers **
      ** Customer reviews have the strongest positive relationship with monthly purchases **
      ** Product ratings alone have little influence on purchase volume **
      ** Product availability is consentrated within a few dominant catagories **
